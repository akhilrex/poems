There was no real joy
Nothing to really look forward to
No reason to be a better man
no cause to be patient
or even civil
Days, months and years
were all going on in a loop
Work, eat, drink, repeat
The world didn't care
and neither did I
about what we did to each other
Rut - some would say
So be it
It was still all
a thousand times bearable
than tiny arms clutching at me
and teary eyes pleading me
to not leave her
to go to work
