I Growl when I am growled at
snarl when I am shown teeth
I fret and curse and get loud
whenever she does a “bad” deed
The thought that I am losing
that too against a child
Love, compassion, threat and fear
no effective method I can find
And yet my biggest failure
is losing sight of the idea
that I can’t really be the best parent
by just reading books
