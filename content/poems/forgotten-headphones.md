I forgot to bring my headphones
And now I HAVE to listen
To that young couple
trying to impress
Every woman of that group
talking about her dress
that one irate customer
asking for redress
that mother speaking in anger
so unable to suppress
All this and the sound of rain
It’s not too bad - I must confess
