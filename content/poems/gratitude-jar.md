Someone brought a porcelain jar
white, delicate - just about right
Another brought one made of glass
almost crystal - shiny and bright
Cardboard boxes and plastic jugs
Metal tins and coffee mugs
It doesn’t take long to see through the farce
It's not about gratitude, only the jars.
