This man
who has to work all day
just to make what I'll pay for this one coffee
who just can't escape his feudal system
From one taskmaster to another.
Utterly replaceable
this faceless and nameless man
who is expected to just accept his fate
be born, toil, reproduce and die
with nary a glimpse of the delights
the human condition can afford
Just be and accept what he is
a resource - human resource.
has every right to
actually burn everything down.
