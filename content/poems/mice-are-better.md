Every ding, every chat, every email
I don't want to reply immediately
I needn't be connected every time
to know what's happening
in the lives of people I don't personally know
in the politics of villages I don't visit
the amazing, astonishing feats people perform
on the plates of people travelling the world
and yet my hand itself starts reaching
for this rectangular trap
that I know is a trap
because I am full of fear - inside
of the FOMO
and the idea of being alone with my thoughts
of being labeled that weirdo
who doesn't know who won the game
and sits alone silently
smiling at nothing specific
I have let these digital faes
alter my instincts
maybe even my DNA
Mice are better
