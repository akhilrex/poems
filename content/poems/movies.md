Movies should be fantasy
dragons, magic and orcs
Or unreal action fests
flying kicks and bullets galore
Sci-fi also does the job
spaceships, aliens or robots
Slapstick comedy is fine too
parodies, gags and the wordplay they do
Just keep the “real” movies away
about the human condition and life in grey
“Escape” - this is all I seek
My life is already too real anyway
