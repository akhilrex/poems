Why wake up
Why do anything
Why travel far to see that sight
or eat that dish all are raving about
Why work and why even take breaks
Why meet people and why celebrate
Nothing is great, Nothing is worse
Everything is - Just Because
Why be happy
Or even work to try to be
Why even be sad
Why feel any emotion at all
When not a lot is in control
Why earn, why spend
If this cycle never ends
Why have a purpose
And what if it’s completed
Why run in this race against self
which I will never win
If this is what burnout feels like
I think I am burnt out from life.
